i'm a full-stack developer, southern california based who loves website development.

I have always been attracted to the road less traveled. For high school I stepped away from the traditional experience and into Da Vinci Charter Academy. At the time, Da Vinci was one of eight schools funded by Bill Gates to promote project-based learning while emphasizing technology. I threw myself into an outside the box frame of mind, working towards the completion of projects, setting expectations and maximizing efficiency through technology.

At Cal-State Northridge I discovered my passion for learning. I graduated top-ten in Economics with multiple semesters on the dean’s list. The Monday after graduation I entered the full-time workplace, starting a position in accounting. After a year I felt unchallenged, I made the switch to my position at Petrol managing their media accounting and all accounts receivables. I am truly thankful for my time at Petrol, but something was still missing.

I began taking classes, C++ and calculus. In pursuit of master’s programs, I discovered bootcamps. Comparing them, a masters might be one to two years and tens to hundreds of thousands of dollars vs. a bootcamp which is three to six months at a fraction of the cost. Efficiency is key in my life, as I researched bootcamps they became increasingly more attractive. Fast-forward to now and I have successfully completed my education at LearningFuze bootcamp.

I worked twelve-hour days, more than I ever worked at anything prior. Every day I found myself looking for more time to code. Activities and projects exposed gaps in my knowledge that I strived to fill. I live to learn and without it I get bored. LearningFuze has taught me to be confident in making mistakes, learning from them and working to become a better me.

Looking down the line, I want to always be learning, solving problems and helping guide others through their careers. At the end of the day, I want to be at a company I believe in, working with people I admire and advancing my knowledge base.
